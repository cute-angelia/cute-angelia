We use COOKIES  or Cache Data to benefit users. For example, to expedite the login process in our virtual community, you can choose to store your username in a COOKIE or Cache Data . This way, the next time you log in to our service, it can be more convenient and quick. COOKIES  or Cache Data   help us identify the pages and content you access, the time you spend on specific services, and the services you choose.

You can refuse or manage COOKIES, Cache Data   or WEB BEACON through your browser or user preference mechanisms. However, please note that if you disable COOKIES, Cache Data or WEB BEACON, we may not be able to provide you with the best service experience, and some services may not function properly. Additionally, you will still receive the same number of ads, but their relevance to you may decrease.
